World Wine Production 1961-2014

Instructions:

    - Move your cursor over the map area to see data for the country data to display below on the mini graph.
    - You can select a country by clicking on it. You can unselect a country by double-clicking.
    - Selecting a country allows you to move your cursor down to the mini graph and see precise data for each year by moving your cursor along the line.

Description:

This is a map made up of data for wine production by country from 1964-2014. The data output to the map is the total wine production per country for the span of that entire time. The mini graph shows yearly production data for each country.
